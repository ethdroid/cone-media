</> Common questions

New to this? Perfect.
Most of our clients were too.

I've never had a website before. Where do I even start?

Right here. Fill out the form below or give us a call, and we'll walk you through everything — what a website can do for your business, what you'll need (usually just your logo, photos, and basic info), and what happens next. No preparation required.

Do I need to buy hosting or a domain myself?

Nope. We handle all of it — registering your domain if you don't have one, setting up hosting, and keeping everything renewed and secure. You'll never get a scary technical email you don't understand.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most small-business sites are ready within a couple of weeks, depending on how quickly we get your content (photos, menus, business details). We'll give you a clear timeline before we start.

What if I need changes after the site is live?

That's the whole point of working with us. Send an email or a text describing what you want changed, and we'll take care of it — usually the same day. No dashboards to learn, no extra software.

How much does it cost?

It depends on what your business needs — a single-page site is very different from a multi-page site with online ordering. Reach out and we'll give you a straightforward quote with no obligation and no pressure.

Will my website work on phones?

Absolutely — that's non-negotiable. Most of your customers will find you on their phone, so every site we build is designed mobile-first and tested across devices.
